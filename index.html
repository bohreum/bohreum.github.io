Bohreum

An ethereum inspired blockchain for Denmark.

The name Bohreum is a portmanteau of Niels Bohr, Bohrium and Ethereum.

The "ether" in the bohreum network is Bohr.

Ideas, goals and design decisions

  • Byte-code compatable with Ethereum.
  • Fixed number of nodes that verify blocks.
  • Proof-of-stake based instead of proof-of-work.
  • Both anonymous and CPR/CVR backed accounts.
  • A fair compute platform for the Danish economy.
  • Delay some information about transactions in the Mempool to prevent front-running.
  • High level of transparency in all regards (governance, transactions, source code)
  • Support for some amounts of Universal basic income (UBI).

  • The primary purpose of Bohreum is to provide a modern financial infrastructure to Denmark.
  • Bohr is similar to Ether (ETH) in ethereum
  • There will be a need for a native stablecoin in the Bohreum ecosystem (Astrid).
  • The blockchain will not be fully decentralized because it needs government backing.
  • The blockchain will use a poor man's proof-of-stake.
